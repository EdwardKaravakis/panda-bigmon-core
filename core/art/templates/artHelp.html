ART GRID jobs help

The color coding of test result is the following:
  • Active
    - PanDA job still running.
  • Succeeded
    - PanDA job finished, sub-steps results are not loaded yet;
  • Succeeded
    - Athena and sub-steps succeeded;
  • Finished
    - Athena succeeded, sub-steps failed;
  • Failed
    - Athena failed or PanDA job failed;
